When working on a piece of writing it is hard trust your own judgement. Although peer editing overcomes this barrier, it is useless if you have no on else to edit with. Enter Revisely, which allows you to submit an excerpt along with a specific question. When you post, Revisely allows other members of the community to provide feedback. They can highlight any small snippet of text and annotate the piece with relevant and useful suggestions. The powerful but easy-to-use annotation tools make giving and receiving feedback on Revisely superior to any other general question-and-answer site.

If a piece of feedback is helpful, you can reward the author points. The point system provides incentive for everyone to contribute quality feedback. You choose how many points you want to give out when you submit your paper, and that amount is automatically debited from your account. As you give out points, the total on your paper decreases until you finally reach 0. Spend your points wisely, and be sure to contribute to obtain more! The point economy ensures that Revisely will remain a helpful place where anyone can find quality feedback.
